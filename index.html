TOTALLY TRANSFORMED PERSON WITH CHRISTIAN VALUES

We envision a totally transformed person with Christian values practised in the family and community that preserved the richness of Filipino heritage and diverse cultures in harmony with God's creation.

WELL-INTEGRATED AND FAMILY-ORIENTED PERSONS

As members of SLCD family steadfast to our faith in God, guided by the Blessed Virgin Mary and San Lorenzo Ruiz, we commit ourselves to form well-integrated and family-oriented persons, and to exercise inspired leadership in the pursuit of academic and religious excellence.

ENRICH AND IMPART FILIPINO SOCIO-CULTURAL TRADITION

In order to live and pursue with our mission, we dedicate all our human resources to enrich and impart Filipino socio-cultural tradition and to empower families to participate in building a Christian environment.

WHY STUDY IN SAN LORENZO COLLEGE OF DAVAO?

San Lorenzo College of Davao, Inc., as a Filipino and Catholic school, delivers outstanding educational opportunities through its Christian tradition of being a loving, supportive, and caring community. The academic progress of your child is our number one priority and we are committed to display enthusiasm, dedication and high level teaching skills. We persistently do our best to build an environment in which every student is supported to reach the highest levels of success.

The foundation of our learning environment is the positive relationships between parents, students and teachers and as we encourage constant communication to ensure your child’s success. We welcome you to the San Lorenzo College of Davao, Inc. and we believe that every student can succeed and excel.

Our Programs

PROGRAMS OFFERED

Early childhood education

Focuses on educating children from the ages of infancy until six years old and which includes such programs as nursery school, day care, or kindergarten.

PRIMARY EDUCATION

The initial stage of education and its basic aim is to create, establish and offer opportunities to the early ages of your children.

SECONDARY EDUCATION

A compulsory education that takes place after six years of primary education and is followed by higher education, vocational education or employment.

SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Two years of specialized upper secondary education; students may choose a specialization based on aptitude, interests, and school capacity.
